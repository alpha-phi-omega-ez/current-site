Our Service

During our 75 years we have received the Dean Arno Nowotny, an APO national service award, a record breaking 6 times. Our chapter has a huge focus on helping our community and always have a focus on service. We currently run the campus Lost and Found, Backtest Service, and Charger Loan Service. We are always striving to use our resources and apply our efforts to a diverse set of organizations. Since returning to campus full time we have worked to do over 1000 hours each semester and plan to continue this. As a group we hope our efforts make a difference in our community and are always aiming to use our time to help wherever we can.
