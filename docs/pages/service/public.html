Public Service Events

At APO we love helping our community and we love working with our community on campus to help the Capital Region. We have recently increased our public and joint events. Last year we hosted 2 events at Sanctuary for Independent Media where we worked with over 5 on campus organizations. This year we are hosting Service Day on October 1st where we hope to help any interested RPI student go out in the community and do some community service. We are also always still planning to host at least 1 other public event and are always looking to do joint events with other organizations. We do many service events a week and we are more the happy to invite you along or help you host an event for your members, just reach out to [email protected] and if you are an individual interested in doing more with us you may want to consider joining!
